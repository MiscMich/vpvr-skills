## The JOLT Effect (Overcoming Indecision)

**Key Insight**: 40-60% of deals are lost to customer **indecision**, not competitors.

Owners often want to switch but fear making the wrong choice. The JOLT framework addresses this:

| Letter | Skill | VPVR Application |
|--------|-------|------------------|
| **J** | Judge Indecision Level | "Are you ready to hand over management, or still exploring options?" |
| **O** | Offer Recommendation | "Based on your property, I recommend our Premium tier because..." |
| **L** | Limit Exploration | "You don't need to compare 10 companies. Here's what actually matters..." |
| **T** | Take Risk Off Table | "Start with a 90-day trial. If we don't hit targets, you can exit." |

### JOLT Scripts for Owner Conversations

**Judging Readiness**:
```
"I want to make sure we're the right fit. Are you actively looking to
make a change this quarter, or still in research mode? Either is fineâ€”
I just want to respect your time."
```

**Offering Clear Recommendation**:
```
"Based on what you've told me about your property and goals, I'd recommend
our full-service tier at 20%. Here's specifically why this is best for
your 4-bedroom waterfront..."
```

**Limiting Overwhelm**:
```
"There are 50+ property managers in the Keys. But honestly? Only 3 things
matter for a property like yours: local presence, pricing expertise, and
communication. Let me show you exactly how we compare on those three."
```

**Removing Risk**:
```
"I understand switching managers feels risky. Here's what I can offer:
a 90-day trial period with a revenue guarantee. If we don't increase
your income by at least 15%, you can walk away with no penalty."
```

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response Framework |
|-----------|-------------------|
| **"20% is too high"** | "I understand - let's look at the math. At 20% of higher revenue vs. 15% of lower revenue, which puts more money in your pocket?" |
| **"I can manage it myself"** | "Many owners start that way. What's your time worth per hour? Most owners we work with were spending 15-20 hours/month on management." |
| **"I'm happy with my current manager"** | "That's great to hear. Out of curiosity, what's your current occupancy rate? I'd be happy to share how that compares to similar properties." |
| **"I want to wait and see"** | "I respect that. What would you need to see to feel confident making a change? I'd be happy to provide that information." |
