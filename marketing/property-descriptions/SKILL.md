## Copywriting Frameworks

### The "So What?!" Formula (GuestHook)

Transform features into emotional benefits by asking "So what?" until you reach the feeling:

| Feature | So What? | So What?! | Emotional Payoff |
|---------|----------|-----------|------------------|
| 70ft dock | Can dock your boat | No marina fees | Wake up, walk 10 steps, and be fishing |
| Heated pool | Swim year-round | Kids stay entertained | More relaxation time for parents |
| 500 Mbps WiFi | Fast internet | Can work remotely | Extend your vacation guilt-free |
| Chef's kitchen | Can cook meals | Save money on dining | Cook with your family like you're home |

### PAS Framework (Problem-Agitate-Solution)

**Structure**:
1. **Problem**: Identify guest pain point
2. **Agitate**: Emphasize what they're missing
3. **Solution**: Your property solves it

**Example**:
> Tired of vacation rentals that promise waterfront but deliver a distant glimpse of water? (Problem) You've scrolled through hundreds of listings, only to arrive and find the "ocean view" requires binoculars. (Agitate) At Blue Pearl, step from your bedroom directly onto a 70-foot dock where dolphins swim by during your morning coffee. (Solution)

### AIDA Framework (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

**Structure**:
1. **Attention**: Hook with unique feature
2. **Interest**: Expand with benefits
3. **Desire**: Paint the experience
4. **Action**: Clear booking CTA

**Example**:
> **Attention**: 70 feet of dock, zero bridges to the ocean.
> **Interest**: This is what fishing vacations in the Keys are made of‚Äîdeep-water access, no navigation hassles, and tarpon that swim right past your morning coffee spot.
> **Desire**: Imagine casting a line at sunrise without leaving your backyard, then grilling your catch on the outdoor kitchen while the kids splash in the heated pool.
> **Action**: Book direct and save 15-20% on your Florida Keys escape.

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Airbnb (2,000 character limit)

**Requirements**:
- Title: 50 characters max
- Hook in first line (mobile preview)
- Casual, conversational tone
- 3-5 emojis (strategic, not excessive)
- Experience-focused over feature-focused
- End with CTA

**Title Formula**:
`[Unique Feature] + [Location] + [Key Amenity]`
Example: "Waterfront Paradise | 70ft Dock | Heated Pool"

**Don't**:
- Start with property name
- Use ALL CAPS
- Overuse emojis (5 max)
- Write walls of text

### VRBO (4,000 character limit)

**Requirements**:
- Title: 80 characters max
- More detailed, formal tone
- Family-focused language
- Safety/convenience emphasis
- Space descriptions
- Practical details (parking, accessibility)

**Title Formula**:
`[Property Type] | [Bedrooms] | [Top 2 Amenities] | [Location]`
Example: "Luxury Waterfront Home | 4BR/3BA | Heated Pool & 70ft Dock | Marathon FL"

### Direct Booking (paraisovacationrentals.com)

**Requirements**:
- Full SEO optimization
- Keywords: [Property Name] + Florida Keys, Marathon FL vacation rental, etc.
- Prominent direct booking savings message
- Internal links to blog content
- Schema-ready structure

**Must Include**:
